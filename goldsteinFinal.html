Understanding Flood Inundation:
A Look into Harvey

How to Use

Use the tabs and dropdown menu at the top of the page to explore features related to flood inundation in the Woodlands, Texas including:

  • Showing the hotspots of flood inundation
  • Landcover in the area
  • And, something else

The data used in this application was created and used for a study looking at the Woodlands three development phases and how each phase performed during Hurricane Harvey. More information on this project and the data can be found under the About tab on the navigational bar.
