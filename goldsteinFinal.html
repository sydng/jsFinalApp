Understanding Flood Inundation:
A Look into Harvey

The data displayed in this application was created and used for a study that explored how the three development phases of the Woodlands performed during Hurricane Harvey.
How to Use

Click on the tabs at the top of the page to explore features related to flood inundation in the Woodlands, Texas.

  • The About tab gives information on the app.
  • Learn more about the associated project under the Resources tab.
  • Click on the Explore tab to interact with the data.

Resources

This data was used in a study by the McHarg Center that explained flood inundation in the Woodlands, TX post Hurricane Harvey. More information on the McHarg Center and the project can be found here.
